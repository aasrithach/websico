Chicken Wings


These crispy chicken wings are the perfect appetizer for any gathering, coated in a delicious sauce!

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs chicken wings
  • 1/2 cup hot sauce
  • 1/4 cup melted butter
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Celery sticks and ranch dressing for serving

Instructions:

Step 1: Preheat Oven

Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). Line a baking sheet with aluminum foil and place a wire rack on top.

Step 2: Prepare the Wings

Pat the chicken wings dry with paper towels. Season with garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper.

Step 3: Bake the Wings

Arrange the wings on the wire rack in a single layer. Bake in the preheated oven for about 40-45 minutes, turning halfway through, until crispy and golden brown.

Step 4: Prepare the Sauce

In a bowl, mix together hot sauce and melted butter until well combined.

Step 5: Coat the Wings

Once the wings are done baking, remove them from the oven and transfer them to a large bowl. Pour the sauce over the wings and toss to coat evenly.

Step 6: Serve

Serve the wings hot with celery sticks and ranch dressing for dipping.
