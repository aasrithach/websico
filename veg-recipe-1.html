Grilled Veg Skewers

Delicious and colorful skewers of grilled vegetables perfect for summer barbecues!

Ingredients:

  • 1 bell pepper (red or yellow)
  • 1 zucchini
  • 1 red onion
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes
  • 1 cup mushrooms
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano

Instructions:

Step 1: Prepare Vegetables

Chop the bell pepper, zucchini, red onion, and mushrooms into bite-sized pieces.

Step 2: Skewer the Veggies

Thread the vegetables onto skewers, alternating colors and types.

Step 3: Season and Grill

Brush the skewers with olive oil and season with salt, pepper, and oregano. Grill on medium heat for 10-15 minutes, turning occasionally.

Step 4: Serve

Remove from grill and serve warm. Enjoy!
