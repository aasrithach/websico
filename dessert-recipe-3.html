Cheesecake

A creamy, decadent cheesecake that's perfect for dessert lovers!

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup melted butter
  • 4 (8 oz) packages cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup sour cream

Instructions:

Step 1: Preheat Oven

Preheat your oven to 325°F (160°C). Mix graham cracker crumbs, 1/2 cup sugar, and melted butter. Press into the bottom of a 9-inch springform pan.

Step 2: Prepare Filling

In a large bowl, beat cream cheese, 1 cup sugar, and vanilla until smooth. Add eggs one at a time, mixing well after each addition. Stir in sour cream.

Step 3: Bake

Pour filling over crust. Bake for 55-60 minutes or until the center is set. Turn off the oven and let cool inside for 1 hour.

Step 4: Chill and Serve

Refrigerate for at least 4 hours before serving. Enjoy!
