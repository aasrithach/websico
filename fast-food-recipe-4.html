Pizza


Make your own delicious pizza at home with your favorite toppings. It's fun and customizable!

Ingredients:

  • 1 pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1 cup pizza sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup pepperoni slices
  • 1/4 cup sliced bell peppers
  • 1/4 cup sliced olives
  • 1 tsp Italian seasoning

Instructions:

Step 1: Preheat Oven

Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).

Step 2: Prepare the Dough

Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness. Transfer to a baking sheet or pizza stone.

Step 3: Add Sauce and Toppings

Spread pizza sauce evenly over the dough. Sprinkle with mozzarella cheese and add your favorite toppings.

Step 4: Bake

Bake in the preheated oven for about 12-15 minutes, or until the cheese is bubbly and the crust is golden brown.

Step 5: Slice and Serve

Remove from the oven, slice, and serve hot!
