Lemonade


Refreshing lemonade is a perfect thirst quencher for hot days!

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 4 cups water
  • Ice cubes
  • Fresh lemon slices for garnish

Instructions:

Step 1: Make Lemon Juice

In a measuring cup, squeeze the juice from fresh lemons until you have one cup.

Step 2: Mix Sugar and Water

In a pitcher, mix the sugar with 1 cup of warm water until dissolved.

Step 3: Combine Ingredients

Add the lemon juice and remaining water (3 cups) to the pitcher and stir well.

Step 4: Chill

Refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to chill.

Step 5: Serve

Serve over ice and garnish with lemon slices. Enjoy your refreshing lemonade!
