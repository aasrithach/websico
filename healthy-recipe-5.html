Oatmeal

A warm and comforting bowl of oatmeal to start your day right!

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 2 cups water or milk
  • 1 tablespoon honey or maple syrup
  • Fresh fruits (banana, berries) for topping
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon

Instructions:

Step 1: Cook Oats

Bring water or milk to a boil, add oats and cook for about 5 minutes.

Step 2: Sweeten

Stir in honey or maple syrup and cinnamon.

Step 3: Serve

Top with fresh fruits and serve warm.
