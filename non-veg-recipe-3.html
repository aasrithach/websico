Chicken Tikka Masala


This Chicken Tikka Masala is creamy and aromatic, made with marinated chicken cooked in a spiced tomato sauce. A classic dish!

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb chicken breast, cubed
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 tbsp garam masala
  • 1 can (14 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp ginger, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions:

Step 1: Marinate the Chicken

In a bowl, combine chicken, yogurt, and garam masala. Let it marinate for at least 1 hour.

Step 2: Cook the Chicken

In a skillet, cook the marinated chicken until browned and cooked through. Remove and set aside.

Step 3: Make the Sauce

In the same skillet, saut√© onion, garlic, and ginger until fragrant. Add crushed tomatoes, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 10 minutes.

Step 4: Combine

Add the chicken back to the sauce and let simmer for an additional 5 minutes. Serve hot, garnished with cilantro.
