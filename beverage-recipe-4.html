Mojito


A refreshing mint and lime mojito perfect for hot summer days!

Ingredients:

  • 10 fresh mint leaves
  • 1/2 lime, cut into 4 wedges
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 cup ice cubes
  • 1 1/2 ounces white rum
  • 1/2 cup soda water

Instructions:

Step 1: Muddle Mint and Lime

In a glass, muddle mint leaves and 2 lime wedges with sugar to release the mint oils and lime juice.

Step 2: Add More Lime

Add 2 more lime wedges and muddle again to release more juice.

Step 3: Fill with Ice

Fill the glass with ice cubes.

Step 4: Add Rum and Soda

Pour in the rum and top with soda water. Stir gently to mix.

Step 5: Garnish and Serve

Garnish with a mint sprig and enjoy your refreshing mojito!
