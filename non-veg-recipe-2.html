Spicy Fish Curry

A flavorful fish curry that's spicy and aromatic, perfect with rice!

Ingredients:

  • 500g fish fillets
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, minced
  • 2 tablespoons curry powder
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • Salt to taste
  • Cilantro for garnish

Instructions:

Step 1: Sauté Onions

Heat oil in a pan, add onions, and sauté until golden brown.

Step 2: Add Tomatoes and Spices

Add tomatoes, garlic, ginger, and curry powder. Cook for 5 minutes.

Step 3: Add Fish and Coconut Milk

Place fish fillets in the pan, pour coconut milk, and simmer for 10-15 minutes.

Step 4: Serve

Garnish with cilantro and serve hot with rice.
