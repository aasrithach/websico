Beef Stroganoff

A creamy beef stroganoff served over egg noodles, perfect for a hearty meal!

Ingredients:

  • 500g beef sirloin, thinly sliced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cups mushrooms, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons flour
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • Egg noodles for serving

Instructions:

Step 1: Cook Beef

Heat oil in a skillet, add beef, and cook until browned. Remove and set aside.

Step 2: Sauté Onions and Mushrooms

Add onions and mushrooms to the skillet and sauté until soft.

Step 3: Add Flour and Broth

Stir in flour, then gradually add beef broth. Bring to a simmer.

Step 4: Add Sour Cream and Beef

Stir in sour cream and add the beef back to the skillet. Cook for another 5 minutes.

Step 5: Serve

Serve over cooked egg noodles.
