Cheeseburger


This classic cheeseburger is juicy, flavorful, and easy to make. Perfect for any grill master!

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef (80/20)
  • 4 slices cheddar cheese
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 cup lettuce, shredded
  • 1 tomato, sliced
  • 1/2 onion, sliced
  • Ketchup and mustard for serving

Instructions:

Step 1: Prepare the Beef Patties

In a bowl, mix the ground beef with salt and pepper. Divide into 4 equal portions and shape into patties.

Step 2: Heat the Grill

Preheat your grill to medium-high heat and brush the grates with olive oil to prevent sticking.

Step 3: Cook the Patties

Place the patties on the grill. Cook for about 4-5 minutes on one side until browned.

Step 4: Flip and Add Cheese

Flip the patties and cook for an additional 3-4 minutes. Add a slice of cheddar cheese on top of each patty and cover the grill for 1-2 minutes to melt the cheese.

Step 5: Toast the Buns

While the cheese melts, place the hamburger buns on the grill, cut side down, for about 1-2 minutes until lightly toasted.

Step 6: Assemble the Cheeseburgers

On the bottom half of each bun, place a patty with melted cheese, followed by lettuce, tomato, and onion. Top with ketchup and mustard, and close with the other half of the bun.

Step 7: Serve

Serve immediately with your favorite sides like fries or coleslaw!
