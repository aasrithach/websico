Iced Coffee


Enjoy a refreshing glass of iced coffee to kickstart your day!

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup brewed coffee, cooled
  • 1/2 cup milk or cream
  • 1-2 tbsp sugar (optional)
  • Ice cubes
  • Cocoa powder or cinnamon for garnish (optional)

Instructions:

Step 1: Brew Coffee

Brew a cup of your favorite coffee and let it cool to room temperature.

Step 2: Prepare Glass

Fill a glass with ice cubes.

Step 3: Mix Ingredients

Pour the cooled coffee over the ice, add milk and sugar, and stir well.

Step 4: Garnish

Sprinkle with cocoa powder or cinnamon if desired.

Step 5: Serve

Serve immediately and enjoy your iced coffee!
