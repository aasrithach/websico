Grilled Chicken

A delicious grilled chicken recipe that's juicy and packed with flavor!

Ingredients:

  • 4 chicken breasts
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

Step 1: Marinate Chicken

In a bowl, combine olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, paprika, salt, and pepper. Add chicken and marinate for at least 30 minutes.

Step 2: Preheat Grill

Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.

Step 3: Grill Chicken

Place marinated chicken on the grill. Cook for 6-7 minutes on each side until fully cooked.

Step 4: Serve

Let rest for a few minutes before slicing. Serve with your favorite sides!
