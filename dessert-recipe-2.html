Apple Pie

A classic, delicious apple pie recipe that's perfect for any occasion!

Ingredients:

  • 6 cups thinly sliced apples
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 tsp ground nutmeg
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 double-crust pie dough

Instructions:

Step 1: Preheat Oven

Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C). Roll out half of the dough and place it into a 9-inch pie dish.

Step 2: Prepare Filling

In a large bowl, mix apples, sugar, flour, cinnamon, nutmeg, and lemon juice. Pour into pie crust.

Step 3: Add Top Crust

Roll out the remaining dough and place it over the apple mixture. Crimp the edges and cut small slits for steam to escape.

Step 4: Bake

Bake for 40-45 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the filling is bubbling. Let cool before serving.
