Shrimp Scampi

A quick and easy shrimp scampi that’s packed with garlic and butter flavor!

Ingredients:

  • 500g shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 4 tablespoons butter
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 1/2 cup white wine
  • 1/4 cup parsley, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Lemon wedges for serving

Instructions:

Step 1: Sauté Garlic

In a skillet, melt butter over medium heat. Add garlic and red pepper flakes, sauté for 1-2 minutes.

Step 2: Cook Shrimp

Add shrimp to the skillet, season with salt and pepper, and cook until pink.

Step 3: Add Wine and Parsley

Pour in white wine and stir in parsley. Cook for another 2-3 minutes.

Step 4: Serve

Serve hot with lemon wedges over pasta or rice.
